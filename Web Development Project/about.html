A Life of Adventure About My Experience

From a young age, I was always jealous as I saw my dad travel all over the world for his job. It wasn't until I was a junior in high school when I went to Europe for the first time. I particularly fell in love with the architecture and the charm of Paris and knew from then on that I was addicted. Since then, I have traveled to over 20 countries in 4 different continents.

Traveling has given me new perspectives on the world and I hope to inspire others to do the same. I hope to one day work with people on an international scale and one day start my own non-profit to give back to people in need all around the world.
