London United Kingdom

Things to do: Westminster Abbey, London Tower, Big Ben, Ride the London Eye, Visit Tower Bridge, Take a Picnic at Hyde Park, Visit Buckingham, Explore Piccadilly, Go See a Play at West End. Less Than an Hour Away is the Harry Potter Studio for all Potterheads.

Paris France

For the best views of the city go to the top of the Arc de Triomphe or climb to the top of the Sacre Coeur Cathedral in Montmartre. Florence, Rome, Pisa Italy

Visiting the eternal city of Rome is a must when in Italy, but not enough people talk about Florence and the surrounding area. Going to Pisa is cool but even better in that region is the cities of Sienna and San Gimignano. If deciding between Cinque Terre and the Amalfi coast, go to Cinque Terre and enjoy the beautiful views and hikes!

Salzburg, Vienna Austria

If you are looking to spend Christmas abroad, look no further than Austria. Vienna alone has dozens of Christmas markets of various sizes. Salzburg is also a charming place to visit at the same time of year. When travelling between the two cities, make sure to stop in the Alpine village of Halstatt.

Schwangau Germany

The south of Germany is beautiful with all of the snow capped Alps mountains. One of the most magnificent things to visit is the famous Neuschwanstein Castle. Don't skip out on an opportunity to visi this stunning interior.

Amsterdam Netherlands

Every street in Amsterdam is absolutely beautiful. Make sure to take a canal cruise to experience the city from a unique perspective. Rent a bike while you're there and enjoy the beautiful scenery. Make sure to get tickets for Anne Frank's house months in advance, it is a somber yet amazing experience.
