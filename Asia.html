Delhi, Agra, Jaipur India

India is a very chaotic place but is still remarkably beautiful. When deciding where to visit, the Taj Mahal in Agra is a must, as is visiting the pink city of Jaipur. When visiting the Taj, wake up early to be there during the sunrise. It is closed on Fridays so factor that into planning and make sure to watch the sunset from the river the night before.

Bangkok, Krabi, Chiang Mai Thailand

Thailand is remarkable, it is peaceful, beautiful and the food is amazing. For the best temples, make sure to visit Bangkok, for the best beaches, make sure to visit Krabi and Phuket, and for the best elephant reserves, be sure to visit Chiang Mai.

Luang Prabang Laos

Taking a river cruise down the Mekong River to get to Luang Prabang is a unique and amazing experience. When in Luang Prabang, make sure to rent a bike to go visit waterfalls and drive around the city. Visiting the Pak Ou Caves is also unique for its thousands of Buddha statues in one cave.

Hanoi, Ninh Binh, Hoi An Vietnam

A lot of people go to Vietnam and see the Ha Long Bay, while this is beautiful what you can't miss is Ninh Binh. Rent a bike and spend a few days there to explore the mountains and temples there.

Siem Reap Cambodia

When visiting Angkor Wat, take two days to explore the inner loop of temples one day and the outer loop the next. The inner loop is the most famous and waking up early for the sunrise at the main Angkor Wat temple is well worth the loss of sleep. Getting a bike in the city and taking it around the loop allows you the freedom to explore the dozens of temples as you please.

Tokyo Japan

Tokyo is an amazing place to see when experiencing East Asia. There are beautiful temples and gardens that you can explore.
